About Me

Rudy Mendez is a creative individual focused in the Full Stack Developing field. He was born and raised in Phoenix, Arizona, with a rich Hispanic heritage. His background in years of laborious intense work has instilled a deep appreciation for hard work and dedication.

Rudy's journey in the creative industry began during his undergraduate studies at Arizona State University, where he pursued a degree in Graphic Information Technology. This program ignited his passion for creative endeavors, and he is committed to collaborating with talented individuals worldwide to craft exceptional projects.

Outside of the digital realm, Rudy is driven by his love for cars, engine work, real estate, custom metal fabrication, and competitive driving. By merging his professional path with these passions, he aspires to make a lasting impact in his career while nurturing his creative spirit.
